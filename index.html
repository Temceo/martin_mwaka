Magic of Rubik's Cube

Invention

The Rubik’s Cube is a 3-dimensional combination puzzle invented in 1974 by Ernő Rubik, a Hungarian architect and professor. The standard cube is 3 × 3 × 3. It has 3 layers along each axis. Each face is a solid colour when solved: traditionally white, yellow, red, orange, blue, and green.

Progression

Since its original inception the rubik's cube has grown in popularity spawning variations in colour and shapes and sizes.

Timeline

  • 1974 Rubik invents the cube in Hungary
  • 1980 Global launch by Ideal Toy Corp
  • 1981 Gains worldwide popularity
  • 1990s Introduction of speed cubing
  • 2004 World Cube Association founded
